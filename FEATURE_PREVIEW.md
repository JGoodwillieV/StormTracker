### Key Visual Elements

1. **Team Record Entry** (Gold/Orange)
   - Gradient background from yellow-600 to orange-600
   - Star icon (â­) in white circle with orange background
   - Bold white text
   - Shows record holder's name in parentheses
   - Positioned in sorted order (fastest to slowest)

2. **Swimmer's Best Time** (Blue)
   - Solid blue background (blue-600)
   - Clock icon (ğŸ•) in white circle with blue background
   - Bold white text
   - Always labeled "Your Best Time"
   - Positioned in sorted order

3. **Official Standards** (Gray)
   - Dark slate background (slate-800/50)
   - Numbered rank in gray circle
   - Standard name (B, BB, A, AA, AAA, AAAA)
   - Light slate text

4. **Footer Legend**
   - Shows what each icon means
   - Helps users quickly identify entries
